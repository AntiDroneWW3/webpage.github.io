PERKS OF OUR MECHANISM:
Security and Defense:

Such systems are vital in safeguarding sensitive areas such as military installations, government buildings, and prisons. By detecting unauthorized drones, security personnel can take appropriate action to mitigate potential threats or breaches.

Event Security:

At large-scale events like sports matches, concerts, or public gatherings, drone detection systems can help in preventing disruptions, ensuring safety, and maintaining privacy by identifying and neutralizing intrusive drones

Privacy Protection:

In residential areas or private properties, drone detection can prevent invasion of privacy or unauthorized filming. It's especially important in locations where privacy is a concern, such as celebrity homes or high-security compounds

Airspace Monitoring:

Near airports or in restricted airspace, drone detection systems assist in monitoring for potential collisions or violations of no-fly zones, ensuring the safety of manned aircraft.
